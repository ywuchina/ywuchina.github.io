Team Lab Research Profile(团队实验室科研情况简介)

More than 80% of the information acquired by the human brain comes from the visual channel, and computer vision technology centered on scene recognition and perception understanding is the core of information processing and artificial intelligence technology. In recent years, based on the core technology of artificial intelligence, the team has conducted scientific research on three-dimensional vision in computer vision, and achieved a series of scientific research results with international influence. The team publishes more than 20 papers every year in top international journals and conferences, such as IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TVCG, IEEE TEVC, AAAI, ACM MM, CVPR. (人类大脑获取信息的80%以上来自于视觉通道，以场景识别与感知理解为核心的计算机视觉技术是信息处理和人工智能技术的核心。近年来，团队以人工智能核心技术为基础，围绕计算机视觉中的三维视觉展开科学研究，取得了一系列具有国际影响力的科研成果。团队每年在IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TVCG, IEEE TEVC, AAAI, ACM MM, CVPR等国际顶级期刊和会议上发表论文20余篇。)

Minimum Requirements for Admission of PhD Students(招收博士生最低要求):

The Master students in our team have strong capabilities and most of them wish to be promoted to PhD in their second year. However, due to the limited and precious number of PhD places, every year some students who have published as student first works in top international journals or conferences fail to get a PhD place in our team. Therefore, for external PhD applicants, we require at least one student first-authored paper in a top international journal or conference in CCF-A category. If you are interested in joining our team for a PhD program and meet the above criteria, please contact us as soon as possible. We offer a rich package for PhD students. (我们团队的硕士生都具备强大的能力，大多数都希望在第二年晋升为博士生。然而，由于博士名额有限而珍贵，每年都有一些曾在国际顶级期刊或会议上以学生一作身份发表论文的学生未能在我们团队获得博士生名额。因此，对于外校申请博士生的学生，我们要求至少发表过一篇学生一作在CCF-A类国际顶级期刊或会议上的论文。如果您有意向加入我们的团队攻读博士学位并且符合以上条件，请尽快与我们联系。我们为博士生提供丰富的待遇。)

How and When to Apply(如何以及何时联系):

  • If you are ready to take your passion for computer science to the next level, please reach out to us for more information and to express your interest. Contact us at ywu[at]xidian.edu.cn to learn more about the application process and how you can become a part of our thriving research team. (如果您准备好将自己对计算机科学的热情提升到新的高度，请联系我们了解更多信息并表达您的兴趣。请通过ywu[at]xidian.edu.cn联系我们，了解更多申请流程以及如何成为我们蓬勃发展的研究团队的一员。)
  • It is generally recommended that undergraduate students who are eligible for graduate school begin contacting us around June-July of the second semester of their junior year. We will arrange the assessment in July-August. Due to the large number of applicants each year, the exempted graduate school slots are basically all filled by early August. For students who plan to pursue a doctoral degree, we usually finalize the list of students who will enter in September of the following year by November. In view of the limited number of places and the large number of applicants, we advise students interested in joining our team to prepare well in advance and not to wait too long before contacting us. (一般建议符合保研条件的本科生在大三下学期的6-7月份左右开始与我们联系。我们将在7-8月份安排考核。由于每年报考人数众多，免试研究生名额基本在8月初就已全部填满。对于计划攻读博士学位的学生，我们通常会在11月之前确定下一年9月入学的学生名单。鉴于名额有限，申请人数众多，我们建议有意向加入我们团队的学生提前做好准备，并不要等待太久再与我们联系。)

Work Environment(工作环境):

Our team currently has more than 20 students, creating a strong research atmosphere and providing a superior learning environment for students to concentrate on their research and studies. Our team often organizes fun and relaxing group activities, aiming to allow students to have fun while acquiring knowledge. In addition, our lab offers a wealth of computing resources, including multiple RTX 4090Ti and RTX 3090Ti graphics cards equipped with various server workstations, totaling more than 10. It is worth mentioning that we have recently purchased three new six-card RTX 4090Ti servers to further enrich our computing resources. (我们的团队目前有 20 多名学生，营造了浓厚的研究氛围，为学生专注于研究和学习提供了优越的学习环境。我们的团队经常组织轻松有趣的团建活动，旨在让学生在获取知识的同时享受乐趣。此外，我们的实验室还提供丰富的计算资源，包括多块RTX 4090Ti和RTX 3090Ti显卡，配备各种服务器工作站，总数超过10台。值得一提的是，我们最近新购买了三台六卡 RTX 4090Ti 服务器，进一步丰富了我们的计算资源。)
