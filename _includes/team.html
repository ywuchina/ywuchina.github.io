Enrollment Informations

This year, I am recruiting 3-5 master's students in the Computer Science and Technology program, which is ranked in the top 0.1% according to ESI. Students interested in recommended admission or intending to take the graduate entrance examination are encouraged to get in touch for more details(ywu@xidian.edu.cn). Moreover, our research team has several vacancies for both master's and Ph.D. candidates. You are welcome to join us!

We are looking for individuals who:

  • Have a profound interest in Artificial Intelligence, enjoy conducting in-depth research, and are motivated by more than just the acquisition of a diploma or degree.
  • Are determined to pursue scientific research work after graduation.
  • Exhibit a spirit of exploration and are not discouraged by challenges.
  • Possess strong English language skills.
  • Are mature thinkers with ideals and aspirations.
